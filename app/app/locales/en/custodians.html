Hearth.net custodians are the community of people who look after the content of the generous social network, observing the terms and conditions, and keeping good manners & netiquette in place. They try to help Hearth users with problems, offer advice on using the website and its functionality according to their permissions and abilities.

They are separated into several communities depending on their permissions. Custodians can easily be identified, as they are members of one of these communities:

1) Admins: “Hearth.net – core team“
Admin is the highest position in the Hearth.net administration hierarchy; it is only taken up by users from the core team at Hearth.net, i.e. Adato Paradigma, z.s. association, and is never delegated outside this circle.
2) Custodians: “Hearth.net – content curators”
Every custodian always adheres to the valid terms and conditions, and acts in accordance with their best intentions. This user activity is performed willingly to help out Hearth.net on a volunteer basis.
Custodians are users who are active on Hearth and meet the following criteria:
  • have been registered for at least 1 year
  • have their profile filled in openly and honestly
  • they have personally been in touch with admin members, who give them temporary permissions for Hearth.net administration
  • by communicating with admins, they have demonstrated they are capable of being custodians
Permissions and responsibilities of Hearth custodians:
  • to pause or stop gifts or wishes breaching Hearth.net terms and conditions
  • to communicate with Hearth.net users when they violate rules, i.e. inserting inappropriate content
  • suggesting and accepting changes to Hearth.net T&Cs in co-operation with Hearth.net admins
  • to communicate with Hearth.net users trying to navigate the application and provide guidance and assistance for using Hearth.net
Means of communication with custodians
  • Custodians always act in both their name and the name of their particular admin community.
  • Custodians communicate with you via messages,
    admins communicate through their registered e-mail or messages.
  • If you disagree with the conduct of any custodian, you can send us an appeal and report their method(s) by e-mail to: support@hearth.net
  • You can give a specific custodian a rating only on the profile of a given community by adding their name to your comment.
How to become a Hearth.net custodian

You can nominate any other user if they meet all the required conditions and you can also be nominated by anybody else. A proposed user becomes a custodian after being accepted into the given community and their designation ends in the case that they leave the community or their membership of the community is cancelled.
