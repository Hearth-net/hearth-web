Who are we

We are a generous social network. Place where you offer gifts and fulfill your mutual wishes. From little to big things, work experience or even life wisdom. You can pass on anything you like and find fulfilling. And everything you need keeps coming back from others. Meet people on the same wavelength and share your joy together right now.

About Hearth

The idea of a network for sharing gifts first came to mind of Libor Malý in 2008 during the time of global economic crisis.

Origin of Hearth name

We had many of questions at the beginning. How to name a place where people are living in cooperation? While seeking the answers, two words came up– Heart and Earth. Both share the same root. And when you connect them you get Hearth.

So we have found the symbol of values that are important to us and that we want to advance.

Hearth.net starts on the internet, but aims to go further. It is our online tool for better working of the gift economy in real world. With its help we want to create genuine connections, relationships and communities and have fun doing it. And with you too. Join in.
